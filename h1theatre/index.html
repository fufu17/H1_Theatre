About The H1 Theatre Forum


A community for everything theatre

The H1 Theatre Forum was built with inspiration from Professor Hyewon Kim and her class Globalizaiton and Theatre. Globalization and Theatre explored the various ways in which contemporary theatre has been created and circulated in a global context. The class consisted of reading plays and relevant academic papers followed by open, in-person discussion of prominent themes in the readings. The discussion of these themes included analysis of theatre and its relationship to political activism, social development, and cultural education.

As two engineering students, we seldom take courses like Globalization and Theatre. However, throughout this semester, Hyewon has sparked our passion for theatre and instilled a a drive to study subjects outside of STEM. Thus, as a lasting memory of our senior year, we've created this website for people around the world to engage in meaningful dicussion of theatre. We hope to open the discourse of Hyewon's Globalization and Theatre to all of the world, beyond just one class or one semester. Thanks for being amazing Hyewon <3 We hope you enjoy!

Allen and Rui
